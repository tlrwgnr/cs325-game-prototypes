Starships

  • High Concept: You command a starship and must defeat your opponent.
  • Player Experience Goals: Some strategic choices and some of the anxiety inducing decisions that come with managing these types of situations when trying to defeat your oppenent.
  • Gameplay: The main aspects of gameplay will consist of choosing a ship and then proceeding to enter into an almost battleship like exchange, except you attack your oppenent using your resources as well as upgrade your ship and such.
  • Prototype Goal: I want to explore creating a turn based game in phaser. Phaser has been great for platformers but I am curious to see how it handles turn based games. I'd also love to eventually get the game to have a kind of Startrek vibe where you're the ship commander, but I guess this would be way more combat focused that Startrek.
  • Features: Some features include being able to pick a ship from a few choices and to upgrade and manage your ship while battling your opponent.
  • Setting/Premise: You are a newly christened captain in the Imperial Republic, you must battle against the opposing forces to prove your worth.
  • Story: The game kind of has a story, most of which I mentioned in the setting/premise section.
  • Target Audience: Casual Gamer.
  • Play Time: Probably 5 - 15 minutes.
  • Strategies: There aren't really a variety of strategies to take on in this game, the premise is very simple but how you build up and choose your ship can differ case to case.
